About Us

An organisation at Macquarie University called Perhimpunan Pelajar Indonesia Australia (PPIA) works to support Indonesian students there. While studying in Australia, the association gives Indonesian students a place to interact, connect, and celebrate their culture. The main goals of PPIA Macquarie University are to help Indonesian students succeed academically, build a sense of community among them, and promote Indonesian culture on campus.

For Indonesian students, the association is essential in building a network of support that facilitates their integration into the university and offers them chances for both professional and personal development. PPIA Macquarie University aims to promote cultural exchange and understanding among its members while improving the overall student experience for its members through a range of events, activities, and initiatives.

In order to foster a diverse and welcoming environment that honours many cultures and customs, PPIA Macquarie University also works with other cultural societies and student organisations on campus. Indonesian students can enhance their educational experience, form relationships with peers from diverse backgrounds, and add to Macquarie University's vibrant multicultural tapestry by taking part in the exchange programmes and cultural events arranged by PPIA.
